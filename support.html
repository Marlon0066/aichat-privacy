Frequently Asked Questions

How do I configure an API Key?

AIChat requires you to bring your own API key. Go to the Settings page within the App, select your preferred AI service provider (such as OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.), and enter your API key and base URL. The API key is stored securely in your device's Keychain and is never shared with us.

Where is my data stored?

All your data — including conversations, AI characters, diary entries, and settings — is stored entirely on your device. We do not operate any servers that store your personal data. When you chat, your messages are sent to the third-party AI service you configured for processing, but we do not retain or have access to that data.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual conversations or AI characters directly within the App by swiping or using the delete option. To remove all app data at once, simply uninstall AIChat from your device. Since no data is stored on our servers, uninstalling the App completely removes all associated data.

Which platforms does AIChat support?

AIChat is available on iOS, macOS, and visionOS. The App is built with Apple's native frameworks to provide the best experience on each platform.

What permissions does the voice feature need?

To use voice messaging and voice calls, AIChat needs access to your device's microphone and speech recognition. You will be prompted to grant these permissions when you first use a voice feature. You can manage these permissions at any time in your device's Settings app. These permissions are entirely optional — the App works fully without them if you prefer text-only chat.
