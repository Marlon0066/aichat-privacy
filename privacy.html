Privacy Policy

AIChat — AI Companion Chat App

1. Introduction

AIChat is an AI virtual companion chat application developed and operated by Liang Yue ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use AIChat on iOS, macOS, or visionOS. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws, including the European Union General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

2. Information We Collect

AIChat is designed with a privacy-first architecture. Here is what you should know:

  • No account required. We do not require you to register, sign in, or provide any personal identity information such as your name, email address, or phone number.
  • Local data storage. All your chat conversations, AI character profiles, diary entries, moments posts, and app settings are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is not uploaded to any server operated by us.
  • API communication. When you send a message, your conversation content is transmitted to third-party AI API services (large language model and text-to-speech providers) for processing. The API keys used for these services are configured by you; we do not provide, host, or have access to your API keys.
  • No analytics or advertising SDKs. We do not integrate any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks (no Firebase, Facebook SDK, Google Analytics, etc.).
  • No device tracking. We do not collect device identifiers (IDFA), IP addresses, or any device fingerprinting data.

3. How We Use Your Information

The only purpose of transmitting your conversation content to third-party APIs is to:

  • Generate AI responses to your messages (via LLM APIs).
  • Convert AI text responses to speech audio (via TTS APIs).

We do not use your data for any other purpose. We do not analyze, profile, or monetize your data in any way.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, and Switzerland, the legal bases for processing your data are:

  • Consent: You explicitly choose to configure and use third-party AI APIs. You control when and what data is sent to these services.
  • Contractual necessity: Processing your conversation data through your configured APIs is necessary to provide the core functionality of the App.
  • Legitimate interest: Maintaining local data on your device to provide a seamless user experience.

Since AIChat does not operate any backend servers and stores all data locally on your device, the data processing is primarily controlled by you, the user.

5. Third-Party Services

AIChat sends conversation data to third-party AI service providers that you configure. These may include large language model (LLM) providers and text-to-speech (TTS) providers. Each third-party provider has its own privacy policy and data handling practices. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any API service you choose to use. We have no control over how these third-party services process your data once it is transmitted to them.

Important: When you use third-party AI APIs, your conversation data may be transferred to servers located outside your country of residence, including to the United States or other jurisdictions. By configuring and using these APIs, you acknowledge and consent to such transfers.

6. Data Storage and Security

  • All app data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate any backend servers that store your data.
  • API keys you configure are stored securely in the device's Keychain with the protection level "after first unlock, this device only."
  • Data transmitted to third-party APIs is sent over encrypted HTTPS connections.
  • Since data resides on your device, its security depends on your device's own security settings (passcode, Face ID, etc.).

7. Device Permissions

AIChat may request the following device permissions, all of which are optional and require your explicit consent:

  • Microphone: Used for recording voice messages and enabling voice call features.
  • Speech Recognition: Used to convert your spoken words into text for AI processing.
  • Calendar & Reminders: Used to read your schedule and create events or reminders through AI conversations.
  • Photo Library: Used to select images for avatars, chat wallpapers, and diary entries.
  • Notifications: Used to send you message reminders and alerts.

You can revoke any of these permissions at any time through your device's Settings app.

8. Your Rights

For All Users

Regardless of your location, you have the right to:

  • Access, view, and manage all your data directly within the App.
  • Delete any or all of your data at any time through the App's storage management settings.
  • Revoke device permissions at any time through your device's Settings.
  • Stop using any third-party API at any time by removing your API configuration.

For Users in the EEA, UK, and Switzerland (GDPR)

Under the GDPR, you have additional rights including:

  • Right of access: You can access all your data directly on your device at any time, as all data is stored locally.
  • Right to rectification: You can edit your profile, conversations, diary entries, and other content at any time within the App.
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten"): You can delete individual items or all app data through the App's storage management, or by uninstalling the App.
  • Right to data portability: Since all data is stored locally on your device, you already have full possession and control of your data.
  • Right to restriction of processing: You can stop data processing at any time by removing your API configuration or not sending messages.
  • Right to object: You may stop using the App at any time. No data processing occurs unless you actively send messages.
  • Right to withdraw consent: You can withdraw consent by removing API keys, revoking device permissions, or uninstalling the App.

Since we do not operate any servers or store your data, most rights can be exercised directly within the App without needing to contact us. If you have any concerns, you may also contact your local data protection authority.

For California Residents (CCPA / CPRA)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act and the California Privacy Rights Act, California residents have the following rights:

  • Right to know: We have disclosed all categories of personal information collected in this Privacy Policy. In summary: we collect no personal information on our servers.
  • Right to delete: You can delete all your data through the App or by uninstalling it. We have no server-side data to delete.
  • Right to opt-out of sale: We do not sell, share, or disclose your personal information to third parties for monetary or other valuable consideration. There is nothing to opt out of.
  • Right to non-discrimination: We do not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights.

Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information: AIChat does not sell or share your personal information. We do not engage in cross-context behavioral advertising. No opt-out mechanism is necessary because no sale or sharing occurs.

9. Data Retention

All data is stored locally on your device and is retained only for as long as you choose to keep it. There is no automatic data deletion schedule. You may delete data at any time, and uninstalling the App permanently removes all associated data. We do not retain any data on our servers because we do not operate data collection servers.

10. International Data Transfers

AIChat itself does not transfer data internationally. However, when you use third-party AI APIs, your conversation data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your jurisdiction, depending on where your chosen API provider operates. By configuring and using these third-party services, you consent to such international transfers. We recommend reviewing the data handling practices of your chosen API providers.

11. Children's Privacy

AIChat is rated 17+ and is not intended for use by children under the age of 17. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used the App, please contact us so we can assist you.
